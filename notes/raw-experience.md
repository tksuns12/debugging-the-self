# Raw Experience Notes

This file preserves the original seed of the experience before it becomes too polished.

## Core realization

While driving, I realized that everything is just happening.

Not only external things.
Not only the road, the car, and the world outside.

What I feel about those happenings is also happening.
My thoughts are happening.
My thinking inside is happening.
Everything is equally just happening.

## Equal happenings

The most painful wound in your heart and someone's pooping on the other side of the Earth are exactly the same happening.

This does not mean they feel the same.
This does not mean they have the same human meaning.
This does not mean pain should be ignored.

It means that, before the mind labels one as “mine” and the other as “irrelevant,” both are simply events appearing in reality.

## Important tone

Do not turn this into a grand claim.
Keep it direct, humble, and precise.

The point is not “I am enlightened.”
The point is:

Something was seen.
It looked like everything, including the one who thinks they are seeing, is just happening.
